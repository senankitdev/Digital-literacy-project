# 📌 Project Overview

This repository contains my **Digital Literacy Project Portfolio**, developed as part of the **CSE0001 Digital Literacy course** at VIT Bhopal University.

The project places me in the role of a **Student Digital Ambassador**, where my responsibility is to promote digital awareness and guide peers in using digital technologies safely and effectively.

Through this project, I explored several important aspects of digital literacy such as:

- Creating awareness resources about digital literacy
- Building a professional digital portfolio
- Practicing coding and collaboration tools
- Learning professional email communication
- Understanding cybercrime threats and prevention

These activities helped me develop both **technical skills and digital responsibility**, which are essential in today's technology-driven world.

# 📖 Module / Task Summaries

## 🧠 Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

In this task, I created an infographic explaining the concept of **Digital Literacy** using **Canva**.  
The infographic includes:

- Meaning of digital literacy
- Useful digital tools for students
- Safe internet practices

This task helped me understand how visual resources can effectively communicate digital awareness.

---

## 🌐 Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio

I created professional profiles on:

- **GitHub**
- **LinkedIn**
- **Kaggle**

These platforms help students build an online presence, showcase projects, collaborate with others, and connect with professionals in the technology industry.

Over the next four years, I plan to upload my projects, assignments, and technical work on these platforms to build a strong professional portfolio.

---

## 💻 Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms

For this task, I explored two types of platforms:

### Coding Practice
I completed a beginner-level coding challenge on **HackerRank**, which helped me improve logical thinking and understand how coding platforms evaluate solutions.

### Collaboration Tool
I created a **Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz** using **Google Forms** and collected responses through Google Sheets.

These tools demonstrate how digital platforms support both **learning and collaboration**.

---

## 📧 Task 4 – Professional Email & Digital Etiquette

In this task, I wrote two professional emails:

1️⃣ Requesting an **assignment deadline extension**  
2️⃣ Expressing interest in a **summer internship opportunity**

I also created a **Social Media Do’s and Don’ts checklist** to encourage responsible online behaviour among students.

This activity highlighted the importance of **professional communication in academic and professional environments**.

---

## 🔐 Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

For the cybercrime awareness activity, I studied **UPI / Online Payment Fraud**, which is one of the most common cyber threats in India.

The case study explains:

- How fraudsters trick victims
- Who is commonly targeted
- The consequences of cybercrime

I also prepared a **Stay Safe Online checklist** with preventive measures.
